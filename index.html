At a glance

Here at ECHO, we are driven by a single goal; to establish a stronger, valuable bond between our partner charities, not-for-profit organisations and the real changemakers, YOU.

We strongly believe in by creating bridges between our friends we could achieve a truly open, collaborative society which will lead us towards a sustainable, equal and prosperous future.

Us

ECHO is an innovative Eco Community Hub Organisation which grants greater visibility for our small and medium sized partner organisations enabling them to reach out for the majority of citizens like never before.

We are here to disrupt the status quo within the sector and to inspire people to actively participate in fighting against pressing issues which are drifting our world and its inhabitants into disasters. Doing that by sitting on the couch or volunteering, offering financial support or expertise, acting globally or locally; the point is that we need to act now.

You

To achieve our noble goals, we will need a few crucial ingredients to succeed.

Most importantly: YOU. Your thoughts, your opinion, your creativity, your dedication, your passion. You are the real superhero, did you know that? You have the all the capabilities to change the world completely. Your voice matters the most, so let us amplify it for you. Join us on an extraordinary journey, and your reward surely won’t be forgotten.

Together

World changers are the charities, working tirelessly to better people’s lives; they share the highest level of dedication, compassion and readiness-to-act which are put in use to reach for good; and extraordinary people like you who want to make a difference, and have fun doing it. Together we are building a community that leverages the power of story and technology to transform our lives.

Our model offers everyone the chance to have a once-in-a-lifetime experience co-created with these great organisations. We share these stories with the rest of the world to invite them on an educative journey and mobilise them to become actively involved.

With this unique unity we are driving new levels of awareness and raising more money for charities.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency

    We understand the confusion when people are questioning where their donations go. We believe that the money should be used where we can make the most impact out of it. Therefore, ECHO hand-picks its partners whom represents the same level of transparency as we do, which is 100%. But in exchange we expect that from you too.

  • We make giving fun and easy

    There are so many organisations working to make a difference, but it’s hard for them to raise money in a sustainable way. Our goal is to make giving as fun and easy as possible, so everyone wins. You get the chance to live your dream experience and charities can spend less time fundraising, and more time making an impact.

  • Storytelling is the heart of ECHO

    A good story well told has the power to open us to new ways of thinking, give us insight into the experiences of others, and ultimately make us more connected.The more connected we are, the more impact we can make together.

  • We are all equal friends

    If you thought that we take your money and let you walk off easily… well I have to let you down. We strive to create strong bonds with our partners and members, so it is more like a friendship than anything else. We welcome anyone from anywhere with any background; we believe that we were all born equal and everyone has to be treated this way.

  • Knowledge is power - but it needs some translation sometimes

    We realised that important, life-changing information are circulating in certain “bubbles”, and remain there without being shared or translated correctly to the public. We are here to pop those “bubbles” and let the information flow in its pure, easily understandable form. Our presented journeys are not only entertaining but equally insightful and educative as well.

  • Video contents are the present, not the future

    Now we live in an era of compact information of video contents which needs to be implemented into all kinds of businesses, including charities and social enterprises operate and convey information to the public.

    We also believe we are pioneers in creating a video sharing forum provided for our members which is the most innovative, efficient and transparent way of exchanging thoughts and personal experiences amongst all of us.
